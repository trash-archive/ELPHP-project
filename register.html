3. Data Storage and Security

Secure Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information from unauthorized access.

3.1 Data Protection Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols

  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address vulnerabilities

3.2 Administrator Access

Transparency About Access

We believe in transparency. System administrators may access user notes in specific, limited circumstances to ensure the safety and integrity of our platform.

Administrator access to your notes may occur when:

  • Content Moderation: Investigating reports of Terms of Service violations

All administrative access is logged and monitored. Administrators are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are trained in privacy and security best practices.
